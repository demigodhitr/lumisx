Roadmap

January 2019 - The Birth of Lumis X

Three visionary entrepreneurs embarked on a mission to revolutionize the trading landscape. Their goal: to make trading accessible to everyone, everywhere, while reducing reliance on traditional financial institutions.

September 2022 - Visual FX trading platform added

The first iteration of Lumis X: an online trading platform that makes trading easy to understand, and even fun, using graphic representations for various financial instruments.

May 2023 - Lumis X Trading Platform Goes Live

Lumistakes unveiled its state-of-the-art trading platform, Lumis X. Designed for traders of all experience levels, the platform featured advanced tools and a user-friendly interface, empowering users to trade financial assets seamlessly from anywhere in the world.

July 2023 - Expansion into NASDAQ Stocks

Lumis X expanded its offerings by integrating a diverse range of stocks from the NASDAQ exchange. This addition allowed traders and investors to further diversify their portfolios by accessing the global stock market alongside commodities, cryptocurrencies, and other assets.

The new Lumis X platform was launched, combining the advanced trading capabilities of WebTrader with the collaborative features of OpenBook. This unified interface delivered an intuitive and innovative experience for both individual and social traders.

About Crypto White Paper

A white paper is a type of informational writing frequently utilized in business. It can be used to advertise a company's goods or services and is often utilized to provide a thorough examination of a certain topic or problem. White papers can be several pages to dozens of pages long and are typically rather lengthy.

White papers on cryptocurrencies are comparable to typical white papers, except, as their name implies, they focus on virtual currencies. These papers often give a general overview of a particular coin or token and frequently contain details on the project's staff, the technology it employs, and its development path. White papers for cryptocurrencies frequently contain thorough information on the coin or token economy.
